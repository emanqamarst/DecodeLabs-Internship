More Than Just a Cafe

At Purrfect Sip, we believe every cup of coffee tastes better with a purring companion nearby. Our rescued cats are friendly, healthy, and love meeting new humans. Come for the latte — stay for the love.

All our cats are rescued, vaccinated, and well cared for. Visiting us also directly supports local cat rescue efforts.

Cat Cafe Rules

We ask all guests to follow these rules to keep our cats happy and safe.

  • Do not wake sleeping cats
  • Wash hands before entering the cat area
  • Keep noise levels low and calm
  • Flash photography is not allowed
  • No outside food in the cat area
  • Children under 5 must be supervised
  • Inform staff of any allergies
  • Respect the cats — they choose you!💖
